# Terms of Use

**Last updated:** March 2025

## 1. Purpose

This digital questionnaire is used for the structured assessment of your functional situation according to the International Classification of Functioning, Disability and Health (ICF) of the World Health Organization (WHO).

## 2. Eligibility

Use of this application is only possible via a personalised link provided to you by your therapist or treating institution. Misuse is prohibited.

## 3. Accuracy of Information

You agree to answer the questions to the best of your knowledge and belief. Incorrect information may affect the quality of the therapeutic documentation.

## 4. Not Medical Advice

The results of this questionnaire do **not constitute medical advice** and do not replace the assessment of your therapist or doctor.

## 5. Technical Requirements

A modern web browser with JavaScript enabled is required. The signature (step 9) requires mouse or touch input.

## 6. Liability

The application is provided without guarantee of uninterrupted availability. In case of technical problems, please contact your therapist.

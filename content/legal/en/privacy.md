# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 2025

## 1. Controller

The controller responsible for data processing within this application is the treating therapist or the institution that provided you with this link.

## 2. Data Collected

The following data is collected as part of this questionnaire:

- **Anonymous participant ID** (auto-generated, e.g. TN-847293)
- **Age** (as a number, not date of birth)
- **Gender** (male / female / diverse / prefer not to say)
- **Main diagnosis** (optional, free text)
- **ICF qualifiers** (rating of body functions, activities and environmental factors)
- **Therapy goals** (described by you)
- **Aid wishes** (free text, optional)
- **Handwritten signature** (stored as an image file)

**No names, addresses, contact details or dates of birth** are collected.

## 3. Purpose

The collected data is used exclusively for ICF-compliant documentation of your functional situation by your therapist. There is **no disclosure to third parties** and **no use for advertising purposes**.

## 4. Retention Period

Your data is stored in accordance with legal requirements and the policies of your treating institution. All personal data is automatically deleted after a maximum of **365 days**.

## 5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, rectify and erase your data. Please contact your treating therapist for this.

## 6. Security

The application uses encrypted connections (HTTPS) and stores data exclusively on servers within the EU.
